Valtyn's Pc Repairs

Valtyn's Pc Repairs is a Computer & Laptop repair company that was formed in 2018, by Luthando Valtyn. It started when Luthando Valtyn had different issues with he's first Computer back in 2014(Meccer). The Computer had lots of challenges and that's where Luthando got help from he's older brother to repair the problems that the computer had.After that he fell inlove with fixing Computers and Laptops. In 2019 He did a Technical Support Course at ICollege just to polish up he's skills and knowleged about Computer Repairs.When he was done with the Course he stared to fix Computers and Laptops for money because he was not Employed at that time. The Computer Repair grew so big that he even got a Job as a Technical Support at WhizzICT. He left WhizzICT in August 2020 to try a different feild of Software Development. Currently he's doing Technical Support as the side job and Programming as a Full-Time Job.
